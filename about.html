Our Story

Why we started
Little Genius.

Little Genius Learning & Care Centre began with a simple idea: parents shouldn't have to choose between their child's academic progress and their own family time. Based at North Point Bizhub in Yishun, we built a centre where student care and tuition work together, so homework, SLS, spelling preparation and enrichment are all handled in one place.

Our tagline says it best: leave the academics to us, and enjoy stress-free weekends with your child.

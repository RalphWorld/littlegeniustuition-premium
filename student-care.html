What Is Student Care At Little Genius?

A second home for
your child, every school day.

Structured after-school supervision, P1 to P6

Our student care programme in Yishun gives working parents peace of mind: a safe, clean and conducive environment where children are supervised, supported, and kept on track every single school day. Parents consistently describe the centre as clean, safe and conducive in their reviews.

  • Daily homework supervision, including SLS (MOE's Student Learning Space)
  • Structured study time and spelling preparation
  • Snack and meal time
  • Enrichment and recreational activities
  • A reward system to keep children motivated

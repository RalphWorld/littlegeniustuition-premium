Our Subjects

one consistent standard.

English

Levels P1–P6. Reading comprehension, composition and grammar, with a strong focus on spelling preparation. Our teachers identify each child's gaps and address them systematically.

Mathematics

Levels P1–P6. Concept mastery from fundamentals to problem sums. "In just 3 months, she mastered almost three-quarters of her multiplication tables." — Peggy Koh.

Science

Chinese
